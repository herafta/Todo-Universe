# To-Do Universe

A playful, cosmic single-page app that turns your everyday tasks into floating celestial cards.

## Features

* **Draggable cards** in an infinite cosmic canvas.
* **Zoom** in/out with mouse-wheel or trackpad pinch.
* **Orbit mode** – make every task revolve around the screen’s center.
* **Light ↔ Night** theme toggle (AAA contrast).
* **Auto-load & auto-save** locally; **Backup/Restore** with one click (`tasks.json`).
* **Keyboard & ARIA** accessible, respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
* Built with **Tailwind CSS** – zero build step, just open `index.html`.

## Usage

1. Clone or download the repo.
2. Open `index.html` in your favourite browser (the app is 100% static).
3. Click **+ Task** to add, drag to move, scroll to zoom.
4. **Orbit** to see your universe in motion; click again to stop.
5. Use **Backup** / **Restore** to export or import a `tasks.json` snapshot.
6. Hit the 🌗 icon for Night or Light mode.
